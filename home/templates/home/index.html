Our Story

Hi, My name is Yahna and I am the founder and CEO of T'JON, an all-natural skin & hair care brand which encourages confidence and self love towards your appearance and complexion. We promote skin health because if you look good then you'll feel amazing. This brand tailors all skin colors and hair types to make everyone feel welcome whether you are a loyal customer, a returning customer or even a first time buyer.


I have been formulating my own creams for my husband and 3 children for over 8 years. It all started off with my daughter's skin reacting negatively to supermarket creams. It caused her to have a lot of blemishes around her face and arms. I made it my mission to figure out what was causing these reactions. I found out that it was certain chemicals that shes was using from supermarket creams. That is what led me to exploring ways to create my own natural formulas and ever since her skin has been healed an revitalised.
